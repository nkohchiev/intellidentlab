about dental implants

Dental Implants – The Pros and Cons

Teeth loss is an inevitable incident which can occur due to several factors including trauma, age, tooth decay, and gum disease. When this happens, tooth replacement using dental implants is the best option.

This is due to technological advances in dentistry, which have allowed dental implants to surpass the previous options, i.e., dentures and bridges.
